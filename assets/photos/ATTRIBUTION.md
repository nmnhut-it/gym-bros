**[Free Exercise DB](https://github.com/yuhonas/free-exercise-db)** by Justin Hong (yuhonas)
License: [Unlicense](https://unlicense.org/) — public domain, no restrictions.

The repository's exercises are originally sourced from [Everkinetic](https://everkinetic.com/),
which released its data under public domain.

We use these photos as 2-frame "start / end" position pairs for visual demonstration in the
session player. We chose this dataset because it is one of the few free-licensed exercise
photo collections with real-people demonstrations and ~800 exercises covering a wide range
of bodyweight + stretching movements.

## Note on accuracy

The mapping from our exercise IDs to the upstream IDs is not always exact. For example:
- our `side-plank-knee` maps to their full `Side_Bridge` (we modify by keeping knees down)
- our `plank-knee` uses their full `Plank` (we recommend the modified version on knees)
- our `cat-cow` maps to their `Cat_Stretch` (only cat pose, not the cow opposite)

Where applicable, the tutorial sheet shows a "📷 Lưu ý" caption explaining the difference.
